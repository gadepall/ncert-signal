The first term of an AP is $5$, the last term is $45$ and the sum is $400$. Find the number of terms and the common difference.\\
\textbf{solution:}\\
\fi

x\brak n&=x\brak 0+nd\\
40&=nd\label{10.5.3.5.2}\\
y\brak n&=\frac{n+1}{2} \sbrak {2x\brak 0+nd}\\
